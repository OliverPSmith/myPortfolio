About

--

Hi, I'm Oliver. A London based frontend developer.

Welcome to my portfolio. To negate a compulsive need to constantly re-do my portfolio I have opted for a minimalist style website.

Currently freelancing; the websites I build are completely bespoke, giving your business or project the unique digital presence it deserves.

Despite my minimalist portfolio, I am an admirer of many website design and style trends, and am always keen to branch out and explore creatively.

As a developer I always make sure my code is written in a clear, concise, and reusable manner.

My bread and butter programming language is Javascript, however I am a very quick learner and am always looking to add to my tech wheelhouse.

If you want to get in contact then I'd love to hear from you; you can reach me at: oliver_smith@hotmail.co.uk
